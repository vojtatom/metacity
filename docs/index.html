Metacity

Future without frustration

Cities have become a huge source of data.

All plans and future changes are approved based on models derived from the urban data.

The models help us answer questions and plan future changes.

Local authorities should strive for more interaction between the city administration and the public.

New kind of interaction requires new platform.

A part of this platform is Metacity.

Currently, we use open data from geoportalpraha.cz. We are working on the first prototype, check out:
